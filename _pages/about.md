Hello! I'm Yuejin Xie (谢悦晋), an undergraduate student majoring in Electronic Engineering at [Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)](https://www.hust.edu.cn/). I will join [IIGROUP](https://sites.google.com/view/iigroup-thu/), Tsinghua Shenzhen International Graduate School as a Master's student, advised by Prof. [Yujiu Yang](https://sites.google.com/view/iigroup-thu/).

My research interests focus on **LLM & Agent Safety**, including tool-use safety, agent guardrails, and alignment.

You can find my work on [GitHub](https://github.com/TarferSoul) and [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=mDei3qoAAAAJ&hl=en).
